A project by Kevin Yang, Max Wofford and Aaron Wong.

Hack Club is a registered 501(c)3 nonprofit organization that supports a network of 20k+ technical high schoolers. We believe you learn best by building so we're removing barriers to hardware access so any teenager can explore. In the past few years, we fabricated custom PCBs designed by 253 teenagers , hosted the world's longest hackathon on land , and gave away $75k of hardware.
