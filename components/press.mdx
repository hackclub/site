With over 60,000 teenagers learning how to code, thousands of STEM education programs and hundreds of in person events around the world, Hack Club is actively reshaping the future of the tech industry with a more inclusive and diverse community. 

Hack Club has been partnered with the tech world's biggest companies like [Github](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9lyxsVKGS7M) or [Framework Laptops](https://frame.work/hacktheworld) and STEM education non-profits like [Girls Who Code](athena.hackclub.com) and the [Congresional App Challenge](https://www.congressionalappchallenge.us/).  

If you are writing a story or have other press inquires, please contact Hack Club Co-Founder Christina Asquith: [christina@hackclub.com](mailto:christina@hackclub.com).
